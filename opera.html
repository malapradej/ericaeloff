Atalanta CHOG 2013

... Erica Eloff, who is suitably tall and carried off the role of the prince/shepherd of Meleagro to stunning effect. From the soaring messa di voce of the opening arioso “Care selve” she established her authority and control of the vocal aspects of the role. Her voice is also bright and well-produced across its range, with great power at the top. In the demanding aria “Non sarà poco” which concludes Act I, she displayed great legato and nifty passagework in a high tessitura with a number of high Cs, concluding with a stunning cadenza. - Sandra Bowdler, Opera Britannia
