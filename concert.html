Theodora 2013

Miss Eloff clearly had the measure of the role when she worked with Laurence Cummings back in 2009. Now I think it is fair to say that she has made the role of Theodora her own, so much so that I am reluctant to hear anyone else take on the role, for fear of being disappointed. Her singing was vibrant, even toned, poised and at times technically astonishing, especially in her elegant trills and breathtaking sotto voce. Whenever she was singing, I found myself transfixed in my pew. She clearly has stage charisma too, ... - Miranda Jackson, Opera Britannia
